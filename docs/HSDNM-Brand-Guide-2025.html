Brand Foundation

HSDNM Brand Architecture

A unified, professional design system for the Henry Seriake Dickson National Movement — built on the 60-30-10 color principle with HeroUI (v3 Beta) integration, optimized for political campaign excellence.

01 Brand DNA

The HSDNM visual identity communicates authority, trust, and grassroots energy. Every design decision flows from three brand pillars:

Authority

Strong typography, deep green anchors, and sharp geometric forms signal institutional credibility and leadership.

Trust

Consistent gold accents, clean white space, and structured layouts build the familiarity that earns public confidence.

Grassroots Energy

Approachable body type, generous breathing room,- **HeroUI (v3 Beta)**: State-of-the-art React Aria components with HSDNM branding.ios ensure every Nigerian feels welcome.

Color System

60-30-10 Color Architecture

A three-layer color system derived from logo extraction. Every surface, brand presence, and call-to-action has a defined color role — ensuring visual hierarchy and WCAG AA accessibility across all views.

01 The Ratio at a Glance

The 60-30-10 rule governs all layout decisions. Violating this ratio creates visual noise and weakens brand recognition.

60% Surface
30% Brand
10% Accent
60% — Surface (White / Stone)
Page backgrounds, card backgrounds, white space. Creates breathing room and readability.
30% — Brand (HSD Deep Green)
Header, footer, nav, section backgrounds, filled buttons. Brand recognition layer.
10% — Accent (HSD Gold)
Primary CTAs, active states, highlights, borders, icon accents. Never overuse.

Elevation & Motion

Depth & Animation

Shadow depth communicates z-level hierarchy. Motion communicates intent — transitions are purposeful, never decorative for their own sake.

01 Shadow Scale
shadow-none — Flat
Table rows, list items, inline elements. No depth.
shadow-sm — Raised
Cards, badges, input fields. Subtle float above page.
shadow-md — Floating
Dropdowns, nav drawers, active CTA buttons.
shadow-lg — Overlay
Modals, command palettes, sticky headers on scroll.
shadow-brand — Brand Glow
Primary CTA buttons, featured cards. Coloured shadow.
shadow-gold — Accent Glow
Gold accent CTAs. Use sparingly — one per screen max.
02 Motion Tokens
150ms ease — Hover states, button feedback, focus rings
250ms ease — Nav underlines, badge appears, dropdown open
400ms ease-in-out — Mobile drawer, modal, page transitions
Motion principle: Animate in the direction of meaning. Elements entering slide in from their logical origin (nav from top, drawer from left). Never use bounce or spring on political/official UI — it undermines authority.
